  • Won Idea Pitching Competition | Boston, MA | May 2023

      Fulbright Foreign Students hosted by institutions throughout the United States gathered in Boston for an enrichment seminar on “Public Health in a Complex World: Translating Science, Building Trust.” As medical technologies and treatments evolve, it is more important than ever for experts to communicate clearly to improve outcomes, reduce disparities, and influence public policy to create healthier communities. During the seminar, Fulbrighters participated in scientific communications workshops, conducted site visits to museums and healthcare spaces throughout the city, and learned from industry experts such as: keynote speaker Dr. Ellen Roche, a Fulbright alum and associate professor at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. The workshop, Communicating Science, was organized by Boston University, as a part of the workshop there was an Idea Pitching Competition, like SharkTank, among 14 (100 students) teams of Fulbrighters. Group 09, after a tough competition won the challenge with outstanding teamwork and presentation..
